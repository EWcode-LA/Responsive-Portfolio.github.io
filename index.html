About Me

My name is Seth Miller and welcome to my webpage. My entire career has been in aerospace and defense. I manage multi-million-dollar subcontracts to various companies that provide products to help achieve the end goal for the customer.

I have always wanted to get into coding and recently I was fortunate enough to meet about a dozen hackers who started their own companies. They now support various elements of cybersecurity for a billion-dollar program I am involved in.

Originally after pondering the cybersecurity bootcamp, I chose the coding bootcamp so I can learn the fundamentals of full stack web development. I am excited to share my learning progress and upcoming additions to my portfolio. You can contact me via E-mail or social media listed in the contact me section.
